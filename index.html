About Me

I'm a passionate musician and dedicated student at Columbia University, where I balance my academic pursuits with my love for creating and performing music. My journey combines the intellectual rigor of higher education with the emotional depth of musical expression.

Music has been my constant companion, serving as both a creative outlet and a way to connect with others. Whether I'm composing original pieces or performing covers, I strive to bring authenticity and emotion to every note.

At Columbia, I'm constantly inspired by the diverse community of scholars and artists around me. This environment has shaped my artistic vision and continues to influence my musical development.
